WELCOME TO ALBAY'S MAYON VOLCANO!

Journey to the heart of Albay and stand in awe of Mayon Volcano, a majestic giant and the world's most perfect cone. Towering 2,462 meters above sea level, Mayon's near-symmetrical slopes and frequent eruptions have captivated hearts and imaginations for centuries. Your Albay adventure awaits, promising an unforgettable blend of breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and thrilling activities.

Immerse yourself in the raw beauty of Mayon. Hike or bike through verdant landscapes, taking in panoramic vistas of the volcano from various vantage points. Witness the haunting yet picturesque Cagsawa Ruins, a church partially buried by lava from a past eruption, standing as a testament to Mayon's power. Relax on pristine beaches with black volcanic sand, contrasting beautifully with the turquoise waters of the Albay Gulf.

Delve into the rich tapestry of Albay's cultural heritage. Learn about the fascinating myths and legends surrounding Mayon, woven into the fabric of local life for generations. Experience the warm hospitality of the Albayanos, known for their resilience and deep respect for this natural wonder. Savor delicious local cuisine, featuring fresh seafood and unique flavors influenced by volcanic soil.

Mayon Volcano offers more than just stunning scenery. Embark on thrilling adventures like lava trekking, exploring solidified lava flows, or ATV tours, traversing rugged terrain for breathtaking views. Take a white-water rafting expedition, navigating the exciting rapids of Albay's rivers. Discover hidden waterfalls cascading through lush jungles, perfect for a refreshing dip and a chance to connect with nature. Explore nearby caves and spelunking opportunities, venturing into the heart of the volcanic landscape and uncovering hidden wonders.

A visit to Mayon Volcano is an experience that will leave you breathless. Witness the raw power of nature, immerse yourself in vibrant culture, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Start planning your Albay adventure today and discover the magic that awaits in the shadow of this majestic volcano.

ALBAY'S DELICACIES

BICOL EXPRESS

This iconic dish is a fiery and flavorful stew featuring pork, shrimp paste (bagoong), coconut milk, chilies, and various aromatics. The creamy coconut milk balances the heat of the chilies, while the bagoong adds a unique savory depth. Bicol Express is a must-try for any visitor to Albay, offering a taste of the region's bold and flavorful cuisine.

PILI NUTS

Albay is renowned for its pili nuts, a native delicacy with a rich, buttery flavor. Pili nut candy takes these delicious nuts to the next level, coating them in caramelized sugar for a delightful sweet and salty treat. Available in various flavors and textures, from crunchy to chewy, pili nut candy is the perfect souvenir to bring back from your Albay adventure.
