WELCOME TO CLOUD 9, SIARGAO!

Surfers and thrill-seekers, rejoice! Cloud 9, Siargao's legendary surfing haven, awaits with its powerful right-hand reef breaks and the promise of an unforgettable adventure. Nestled on the island's eastern coast, this world-renowned spot has been luring wave riders for decades, offering something for every skill level.

Challenge yourself: Experienced surfers can tackle the main break at Cloud 9, renowned for its powerful barrels and hollow tubes that will test your skills and leave you exhilarated. For beginners, worry not! Take advantage of the abundance of experienced instructors offering lessons and rentals. They'll guide you through the basics, ensuring your first ride is filled with confidence and joy. If you're seeking a more relaxed environment to hone your skills, head to Saladag Beach, where smaller waves provide the perfect platform for practice and progression.

Cloud 9 isn't just about the waves, though. Witness breathtaking sunrises and sunsets paint the sky in vibrant hues from the iconic viewing deck, creating a picture-perfect moment etched in your memory. Explore charming cafes and restaurants lining the beach, where you can savor delicious local cuisine and refreshing drinks while soaking up the laid-back island vibes. Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere, connect with friendly locals, and experience the unique culture that pulsates through Siargao.

Cloud 9 is more than just a destination; it's an experience. It's a place to push your limits, embrace the challenge of the waves, and forge lasting memories. So, grab your board, pack your adventurous spirit, and prepare to ride the waves of Cloud 9, Siargao. This legendary surfer's paradise awaits, promising an experience that will leave you yearning for more.

SIARGAO'S DELICACIES

SUGBA

This local favorite features fresh seafood, typically grilled over charcoal or coconut husks, resulting in a smoky and flavorful dish. Popular choices include marlin, tuna, prawns, and squid, often seasoned simply with salt and pepper to allow the natural flavors to shine. Served with dipping sauces like vinegar with chilies and onions, sugba is a delightful and satisfying way to experience Siargao's bounty from the sea.

KINAMPAY

This unique dessert is made from grated cassava, coconut milk, and sugar, steamed in banana leaves. The result is a soft, chewy, and slightly sweet treat with a subtle coconut aroma. Kinampay is often enjoyed as a snack or after a meal, offering a refreshing and light way to end your Siargao culinary adventure. Kinampay is a delightful and satisfying way to experience Siargao's bounty fresh steamed kinampay from the island.
