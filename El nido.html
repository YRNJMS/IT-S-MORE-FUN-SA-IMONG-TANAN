WELCOME TO EL NIDO PALAWAN!

Escape to paradise in El Nido, Palawan, where crystal-clear waters, towering limestone cliffs, and hidden coves create a scene of unparalleled beauty. Imagine kayaking through turquoise lagoons, snorkeling amongst vibrant coral reefs teeming with diverse marine life, and sunbathing on pristine beaches fringed with swaying palm trees. El Nido offers more than just breathtaking landscapes; it's a playground for adventure and relaxation.

Embark on island-hopping tours, exploring hidden beaches, secret lagoons, and towering limestone formations. Hike to viewpoints offering panoramic vistas, discover hidden waterfalls cascading through lush jungles, and delve into the vibrant marine life with world-class diving and snorkeling adventures. Beyond the natural wonders, El Nido boasts a charming town with delicious local cuisine, vibrant nightlife, and a relaxed atmosphere, perfect for unwinding and soaking up the island vibes.

Whether you seek an adrenaline-pumping adventure or a tranquil escape, El Nido caters to every desire. This tropical paradise awaits, promising an unforgettable experience that will leave you yearning to return. Book your El Nido adventure today and discover the magic for yourself!

EL NIDO PALAWAN'S DELICACIES

TAMILOK

A type of mollusk found in Palawan, is a delicacy enjoyed by some for its oyster-like taste but considered unappealing by others due to its appearance and slimy texture. It's typically eaten raw with vinegar or lightly cooked. While adventurous eaters might find it interesting, consider sustainability concerns and potential dilemmas before trying it.

DANGGIT LAMAYO

Danggit lamayo, a Palawan favorite, takes the classic Filipino dish "danggit" (salted and sun-dried rabbitfish) to the next level. This delicacy involves marinating the fish in vinegar, garlic, and pepper before partially sun-drying it. The result is a crispy and flavorful dish with a tangy kick, often enjoyed for breakfast alongside garlic rice and eggs.
